  Game Rules & Info


1. You must have a BrainTrain Account to Play.

2. Game starts only once you reserve a ticket for a particular destination

3. Once a ticket is purchased , while on route of the journey or at the destination you will be subjected to various challenges.

4. Sucessfully completed Challenges will be rewarded awards & points which will accumilate overtime or maybe even rewarded a Free Train Ticket if you are lucky!

5. Challenges will vary form simple quizes to picture tile puzzles and many more..

6. Challenges will be about your train destination, the cities/towns they are located, and it's nearby places of interest.
